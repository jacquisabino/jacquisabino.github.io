About Me

Hello World, my name is Lawren Sabino but I just go by Jacqui, sorry for any confusion! I was born and raised in Memphis Tennessee. I moved to Texas in high school and have been here since. I recently graduated with a Bachelor’s in Computer Science from the University of Texas at Austin. I have a passion for technology and labor activism and am constantly looking for ways to integrate advances in technology to every day problems! Please ask and tell me your opinions on AI, information security, and privacy.

Why Computer Science?

I was an okay student. Nothing exceptional and nothing worth noting considering I attended 4 high schools. But I knew how to do my school work and how to do it well. Like many smart girls, I was encouraged to go to college. I chose computers because it felt like forbidden knowledge. Like I could do it all except that. I knew it was not common for women let alone Mexican women. So I figured I'd see what's so special about it. Turns out, a lot. It's a challening world full of complex patterns and methodologies completely foreing to a recent high school graduate. Everything feels broken. Nothing makes sense. And I spent hours talking to my computer, practically begging for it to do what I wanted it to. Most days it felt like my mind was fundamentally different from the genuises who developed Operating Systems, Programming Languages and the architecture in between. And if I'm being completely honest, it still does. But when it works. It feels great. There's a high. A click. The moment you make a connection and never go back. That's what Computer Science is to me. Hill after hill after hill. And I hope one day I reach mount everst. But for now, I learn. I continue to learn how to build, how to write, how to create, and how to share. I want the future to be full of possibility. For the future me's. Who refuse to let spaces dominated by power and control keep them from creating.
